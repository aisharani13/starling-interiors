A work environment that works for you

Work isn’t somewhere you go, it’s something you do. And when you have an office that’s perfectly suited to the way you and your team like to work, it allows productivity to flow in the workplace.

That’s where we come in.

We’re in the business of creating a work environment that works for you. We love the challenge of designing and creating bespoke solutions that are built around the requirements of your company and provide you with a great place to work.

We offer a complete solution to your commercial interior and our expertise have made us a reputable name in the office fit out and refurbishment industry.

Property is the second biggest cost to any business after people. Companies that look after their staff want to provide them with a great place to work, which helps them to produce great work. This is why we make our services inclusive and collaborative as it helps us to achieve an office solution that is perfectly tailored to your business.

About Stirling Interiors

Stirling Interiors is based in Leicester and we are primarily focused on assisting businesses in the Leicestershire, Derbyshire, Warwickshire and Nottinghamshire regions, we cover all of the Midlands regularly however our expansive client base means we have worked on projects up and down the country.

Stirling Interiors has been established since 1975 when we realised it would be a great addition to our office furniture business: Leicester Office Furnishers Limited.

The two companies work perfectly together and enable us to offer the full commercial refurbishment package.
