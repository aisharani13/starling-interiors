SECTORS

We work within a variety of sectors but there are a few that we specialise in, these being offices, educational environments (from nurseries to universities), leisure & hospitality, healthcare environments and government & defence organisations. However, here at Stirling Interiors we will take on any commercial refurbishment project that we have the skills, knowledge and expertise to complete successfully. Please see below for further information…

OFFICES

We specialise in end-to-end turnkey office design. This allows us to review how your staff use your current space, develop an inspiring new office design, and deliver a fit out on time and on budget, with minimal business interruption. We’ll design your office to be a destination where your staff will love working, helping you attract and retain your industry’s best talent.
